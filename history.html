Cave game


Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki
Minecraft, known as "Cave game" in its very early stages, was made by Notch in May 13, 2009.
In this version, you could only place and destroy blocks in a small world.

Indev


Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki

The next version of Minecraft was Indev made in December 23, 2009, which included procedual generation and more blocks.

Infdev


Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki

This version made in February 27, 2010, introduced an almost infinite world. The version also added more blocks, tools, and mobs.

Alpha


Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki

Minecraft alpha made in June 30, 2010, added multiplayer, more biomes, and better procedual generation.

Beta and prerelease


Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki

The final development version of Minecraft, released in December 20, 2010, introduced hunger, more biomes, blocks, mobs, and structures.
Version 1.0 of minecraft was released on November 18, 2011, and the game currently has 22 major updates, with the
current version being 1.21.4.
