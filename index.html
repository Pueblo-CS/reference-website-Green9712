Minecraft is a singleplayer and multiplayer open-world survival game.
Minecraft is played by over 204.33 million people on many different platforms.

Mobs


Image from minecraft wiki

There are hostile mobs in Minecraft that will attack the player.

  1. Zombies move towards the player at a slow speed
  2. Spiders move towards the player at a fast speed
  3. Enderman won't be hostile until you look in its eyes. Enderman can teleport and move some blocks.
  4. Creepers move towards the player and explode when close.
  5. Skeletons have bows and will shoot at the player.

Biomes

There are many different biomes in minecraft that have different blocks, structures, and plants.

    All images below are from minecraft.fandom.com

  1. Plains biomes are the most common, its a flat landscape with trees, flowers, and grass.
  2. Deserts are dry flat biomes that spawn cactuses and dead bush
    Deserts can spawn desert temples with loot.
  3. Ice spike biomes are cold biomes with ice spikes that can have polar bears.
  4. Taiga biomes have tall trees and can have berry bushes.
  5. Cherry grove biomes are biomes with cherry trees and pink flowers.
