how to survive your first day in Minecraft

  1. If you spawned in a plains biome(its ok if you spawned in a different biome), then chop down a tree to get wood.

    Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki
  2. Make sure to kill nearby animals for food, and kill sheep to eventually make a bed with 3 wool and planks.
  3. Open your inventory with the E key and put wood blocks into the crafting grid to make planks.

    Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki
  4. put 4 planks in to make a crafting table and 2 planks to make sticks.
  5. place down and open the crafting table and craft a wooden pickaxe, click the book to craft faster.
  6. find a nearby cave (be cautious of creepers and zombies) and mine the cobblestone, then make a stone pickaxe, sword, and axe.

    Image at the Minecraft fandom wiki
  7. mine 9 more cobblestone to make a furnace, then find coal and iron ore and mine it with your pickaxe.
  8. put coal at the bottom and iron at the top to smelt it. Use the iron to craft armor and tools.
  9. Then you can craft a torch with coal and sticks, torches can prevent dangerous mobs from spawing.
  10. Finally, mine a lot of wood and craft a door with planks, then build your house. you can also craft a bed
    to skip the night. If you don't have a bed, wait until daytime to kill sheep for wool.

Once you complete these steps, then you could explore the world and find loot, or explore caves to find diamonds,
or you can build cool stuff.
